Training Programs

PLP provides training in smart technology and software development, aiming to bridge the digital skills gap.

1MillionDevs4Africa Campaign : The organization has set a goal to train 1 million software developers in Africa by 2027 , as highlighted during its launch in South Africa

Graduation Milestones : By December 2024, PLP celebrated the graduation of 7,859 learners under its Talent Hub program

Global Impact : PLP emphasizes creating pathways for African youth to access global opportunities, aligning with its vision to "take Africa's tech future to the world
